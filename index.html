About Me

I'm a student studying Game Programming at Champlain College, I use Unity and Blender to create a variety of projects and other creative endeavors. This website was made as a place for me to store and share my projects.


  • Portfolio is where you will find all of the art I have created and posted, ranging from 3D and 2D.
  • Games is where you will find all of the games I have created, and posted on a variety of platforms like itch.io and github.
  • Community Service is where you will find all of my volunteer work with the results and/or the website associated with it.
  • Miscellaneous is where I put all of my projects that don't belong to any of the prior categories.

If you wish to get in contact please visit the contact page.
